ALL IPL TEAMS

Sunrisers Hyderabad

SRH started their campaign in 2013 and have played every single IPL after that. They won the IPL once in 2016 defeating the Royal Challengers Bangalore by 8 runs. They have also reached the playoffs every time after 2016 and reached the final in 2018 but failed to win against CSK

Kolkata Knight Riders

KKR has played all the seasons of the IPL, have reached the playoffs 6 times. They won in 2012 beating CSK by 23 runs and won in 2014 betaing KXIP by 3 wickets

Kings 11 Punjab

Kings 11 Punjab has played all the IPL seasons, have come in the playoffs twice and have reached the final once in 2014 losng to KKR.

Rajastan Royals

RR has played all IPL seasons except 2016 and 2017 when they got suspended for two years over a 2013 betting scandal. THey have come in the playoffs 4 times and won in the very first IPL season against Chennai Super Kings in 2008 by 3 wickets.

Delhi Capitals

Delhi Capitals first was Delhi Daredevils but had chnaged their name in 2019. This actually helped them, they came third and second in 2019 and 2020. They reached the final once in 2020 against Mumbai Indians but lost by 5 wickets. They have come in the playoffs 5 times.

Chennai Super Kings

CSK is the only team which has qualified for the playoffs every single IPL except the year 2020. They have also won the IPL thrice in 2010,2011,2018. THey did not play the IPL in 2016,2017 due to a betting scandal in 2013 and were suspended for those two years

Rising pune Supergiants

RPS is a team which was a substitution for CSK. They only played in 2 IPL's and came second losing to Mumbai Indians by 1 run.

Decan Chargers

Decan Chargers played 5 seasons of IPL and won in 2009 against RCB by 6 runs. They have reached the playoffs twice.

Royal Challengers Bangalore

RCB have played all the seasons of the IPL, reached the playoffs 6 times and lost three finals in 2009,2011,2016.

Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians is the best team in the IPL winning 5 times(2013,2015,2017,2019,2020), have qualified in the playoffs 9 times and have played all the seasons.

Gujrat Lions

GL have played two seasons of IPL and qualified once in 2016 and coming their in the same year.

Pune Warriors Inida

PWI played 3 seasons of IPL and did not qualify even once. They played in 2011,2012 and 2013.

Kochi Tuskers Kerala

Kochi Tuskers Kerala played one season of IPL in 2011 in which they came 8th
